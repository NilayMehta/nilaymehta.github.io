Projects

Software for Open Source Prosthetics Arms

Science Fair Project• July 2014 - March 2016

Developed the backend for open source prosthetics on the Arduino(C++) and Raspberry Pi(Python) using muscle contol and voice recongition. Frameworks used include MuSpeech on Arduino and CMU Sphinx for voice recongition. THe project was a tremdeous sucess and won 3rd place at Intel's Intenational Science and Engineering Fair in the Biomedical and Health catagory. The project was also featured in the LA Times and in a telecast called "Think It Up", which was backed by organizations like Staples and the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.

Company Summarize App

Team Project • September 2016

Created a web app as a part of HackGT and can be accessed here . The basis of the app is to save users time during career fairs and gives the user a consise summary of what the company does, relevant buzzwords and industries involved, job listings (based on your selection of desired position like internship or full-time), and recent news stories. All the data collection is done in real time to ensure that the information is up-to-date. My contribution to the group was setting up the project using flask(python based), designing the front end of the app, and helping with data collection for buzzwords and industries the company is involved in.
